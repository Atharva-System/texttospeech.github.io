How to Delete Your Account

Permanently remove your account, including all associated data and settings. Follow the steps below:

  1. Open the app on your device.
  2. Log in to your account if you haven't already.
  3. Navigate to the "Account" tab in the app's menu.
  4. Select the "Delete Account" option.
  5. Read and confirm the warning about permanent data deletion.
  6. Tap "Delete Account" to proceed.
  7. You will receive a confirmation that your account has been successfully deleted.
